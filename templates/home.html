What is Elephant.ai?

Elephant.ai is the savannah's best-kept secret! It's a custom ChatGPT chatbot that adapts to your website content, bringing the power, wisdom, and grace of an elephant to your online presence. Designed for internet publishers and content website owners, Elephant.ai ensures your site's visitors enjoy engaging, human-like conversations while exploring your content.

Can I give my chatbot instructions?

Absolutely! Think of Elephant.ai as your loyal, obedient elephant, ready to follow your lead. You can provide instructions and guidelines to shape your chatbot's behavior, responses, and personality. With mammoth customization options, Elephant.ai will march to the beat of your brand's unique drum.

Where is my data stored?

Rest assured, your data is safe and secure in the Elephant.ai sanctuary. We use state-of-the-art encryption and adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure your data is stored securely. Just like elephants, we take the safety of our herd very seriously.
